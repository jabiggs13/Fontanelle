Skull The Digital Age Estimator of Subadult Skeletons

Bones of the skull

The bones of the skull used for juvenile aging in this project are:

  • Frontal
  • Parietal
  • Temporal
  • Occipital
  • Maxilla
  • Sphenoid
  • Mandible
  • Dentition
  • Compare the bone/element you have to the aging methods provided
  • Input your age range in the "Ages Estimator" box on the right
  • Hit "Save Age Range" if you wish to look at another bone or element
