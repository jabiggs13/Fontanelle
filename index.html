Fontanelle The Digital Age Estimator of Subadult Skeletons

Project Description

This project is aimed at aquainting students of physical anthropology, osteology, and ____ with the different methods used to estimate the age-at-death of human subadult skeletal remains.

Instructions

  • Click on the bone group on the diagram of which bone you wish to assess the age. The groups are:
    • Skull
    • Arms
    • Legs
    • Thorax
    • Pelvis
  • Compare the bone/element you have to the aging methods provided
  • Input your age range in the "Ages Estimator" box on the right
  • Hit "Save Age Range" if you wish to look at another bone or element
